The Magical Trip

One day Manny the elephant, Musù the leopard and Jenny the giraffe find a magic pond were if someone drinks the water from it the animal will swap figures with another animal near him. They didn't know there was a spell so they drank from it and swapped figures. Musù became Manny, Jenny became Musù and Manny became Jenny. What adventure should they face to come back to normal?
