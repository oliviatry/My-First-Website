The Magic Stone

They were astonished about what happened so they went to the “wizard” of Africa. His name was Dudu and he was a lemur, he knew what would happen in the future and how to solve problems too. They went to Dudu and told him about what happened and he immediately recognized what the three animals were talking about. Quickly he raised three wood sticks in the air and said with a low voice the consequences of the magic pond that was exactly what happened to Jenny, Manny and Musù. He told them that to break the spell they had to go to the cave of the evil lion, get the white stone with written “MAGIC”, leave it in the pond and finally drink from it.

Will they come back to normal?

What is the last step they have to face?
